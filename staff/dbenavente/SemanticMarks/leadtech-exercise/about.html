About us

A pioneer in online project management, leadtech was born in 2007 to foster innovation and creativity across a multitude of industries. Empowering clients, employees and investors alike leadtech is dedicated to constant improvement and inspiring new ideas and methods daily. Leadtech strives to provide an entertaining and fulfilling workplace that nurtures positive progress and drives team members to engage their responsibilities with a collaborative and problem-solving attitude. The leadtech vision entails setting the bar high and stepping out of the comfort zone to reach new goals daily and knowing how to celebrate successes!
